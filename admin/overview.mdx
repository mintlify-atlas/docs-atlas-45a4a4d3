The admin dashboard at `/admin` is your central command center for the Levi.dev digital product shop. It requires the `ADMIN` role — any account without that role is redirected away automatically. Once you're in, you get a real-time snapshot of store performance and quick access to every management section from a single screen.

<Note>
  Only accounts with the `ADMIN` role can access `/admin` and any of its sub-routes. To promote a user to admin, run `node prisma/promote-admin.js` (see [User Roles](/auth/user-roles)).
</Note>

## Stats cards

Four summary cards appear across the top of the dashboard, each pulled fresh on every page load.

| Card | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Total Revenue** | Sum of all completed order amounts, converted from cents to dollars |
| **Orders** | Count of orders with `completed` status |
| **Customers** | Count of users with the `CUSTOMER` role |
| **Products** | Total number of products in the catalogue |

The **Products** card is a clickable link that takes you directly to `/admin/products`.

## Revenue chart

Below the stats cards, the revenue chart visualises historical sales from completed orders. You can switch between four time ranges:

- **Daily** — last 30 days, grouped by date
- **Weekly** — last 12 weeks, grouped by ISO week
- **Monthly** — last 12 months, grouped by month and year
- **Yearly** — all-time, grouped by year

All monetary values in the chart are displayed in US dollars. The raw database values are stored in cents and divided by 100 before rendering.

## System status

A **System Status** indicator lives in the top-right corner of the dashboard header. It gives you an at-a-glance signal that the underlying services are running correctly without leaving the page.
